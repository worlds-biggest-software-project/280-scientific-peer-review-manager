# Scientific Peer Review Manager — Feature & Functionality Survey

> Candidate #280 · Researched: 2026-05-03

## Core Features

Primary solutions: Manuscript Central (Clarivate), Editorial Manager, eJournal Press, ScholarOne, Overleaf, Scholastica.

**Must-have**: Manuscript submission workflow, reviewer tracking and invitations, revision management, decision workflow, reviewer plagiarism detection, PDF annotation, communication tracking, audit trail.

**Differentiating**: AI reviewer matching based on expertise, AI-powered plagiarism and image integrity checking, automated desk rejection, recommendation letter integration, reviewer conflict detection, real-time collaboration.

**Underserved**: Transparent review publishing, open peer review models, reviewer performance metrics, predatory journal detection, international reviewer networks, federated journal systems.

**AI-augmentation**: Automated reviewer matching, AI plagiarism and image integrity checking, automated desk rejection scoring, reviewer workload optimization, author revision requirement extraction.

## Legal & IP Summary

Journal editorial policies and peer review methodology are public. No identified patent encumbrances.

## Recommended Feature Scope

**Must-have (MVP)**
- Manuscript submission and tracking workflow
- Author revision management
- Reviewer invitation and tracking
- Review form customization
- Decision workflow and communication
- PDF annotation and editor notes
- Plagiarism and image integrity checking
- Audit trail and manuscript history
- Email notification system
- User management by role (author, reviewer, editor)

**Should-have (v1.1)**
- AI-powered reviewer matching by expertise and history
- Automated desk rejection scoring
- Reviewer conflict detection and management
- Real-time collaboration on editorial decisions
- Integration with institutional affiliations
- Recommendation letter import
- Reviewer performance metrics and analytics
- LaTeX and Overleaf integration
- Integration with CrossRef and DOI systems

**Nice-to-have (backlog)**
- Open peer review publishing workflows
- Transparent reviewer attribution
- International reviewer network building
- Federated multi-journal submissions
- Author revision timeline optimization
- Reviewer recommender system
- Predatory journal detection support
- Integration with preprint servers
- Multi-language support
